Frequently Asked Questions

What topics matter most for US software engineer interviews?

Data structures, algorithms, complexity analysis, and clean problem-solving communication are core requirements.

How should I structure preparation in 4 weeks?

Week 1 fundamentals, week 2 medium problem patterns, week 3 timed mock sets, week 4 revision and weak-topic retakes.

How many coding questions should I solve daily?

A practical target is 15 to 30 quality questions with detailed review of mistakes.
