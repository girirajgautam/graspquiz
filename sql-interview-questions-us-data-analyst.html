Frequently Asked Questions

Which SQL topics are most important for US data analyst roles?

Joins, aggregations, filtering logic, window functions, and query correctness are most commonly tested.

How should I practice SQL for interview speed?

Solve timed query sets and focus on both correctness and readable query structure.

Do I need advanced SQL for entry-level analyst roles?

Strong fundamentals are essential first; advanced features become valuable as role expectations increase.
